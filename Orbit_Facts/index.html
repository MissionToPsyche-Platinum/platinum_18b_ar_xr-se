About

This module visualizes the orbit of 16 Psyche, a metallic asteroid in the main asteroid belt. NASA's Psyche mission launched in October 2023 to explore this unique world, which may be the exposed iron-nickel core of a planetesimal.

Seasonal Fact
Psyche's axial tilt of ~95° causes extreme seasons, where poles face the Sun directly during parts of the orbit.
Mission Data
Psyche is one of the largest metallic asteroids in the solar system.
